## Functionality

*Stutter* samples up to 0.001953125 seconds of audio and repeats the sample up to 50 times. 

|Control|Function|
|-|-|
|A|Sets the length (0 - 0.001953125 seconds) of the sample|
|B|Sets how many times (0 - 50) to play the sample.|
